About Us

The TIME GLOBAL SCHOOL Founded in 2017 by Mr. Ashok Kumar Maurya in Bhadohi district of Uttar Pradesh. TIME GLOBAL SCHOOL provides good quality education, an environment to students to know, create, learn, explore, experiment, analyse and grow with confidence, firmly rooted in our great culture and values. We also put in efforts to sensitize our students to become loving, responsible and promising citizens of India. As a TIME family we are committed to soar higher and higher in the field of education. At TIME GLOBAL SCHOOL we treat each child as individual and give personal attention to shape a well-balanced personality. The school consists of Primary Wing, Secondary Wing, Computer Lab, Library, Art studio, Music and Dance Room, Activity room etc.. The school is focused on the holistic development of the children.
